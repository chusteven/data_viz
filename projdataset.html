Project Dataset

This page is for Steven Chu's final project's dataset in MSAN 622. Source code can be found in my github respository.

Original Location of the Data

The original data comes from the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Data Archive website. I am specifically looking at the National Survey on Drug Use and Health from the year 2013. The website for this particular survey can be accessed here.

License Information

The access notes on the webiste for this particular survey states that the data is freely available to the public.

Types of Data?

The features in this data include a good mix of continuous nomial data as well as ordered and unordered ordinal/categorical data.

Why Did I Choose this Dataset?

I am extremely interested in mental health in general, and especially in the USA. I am hoping to understand what sorts of mental health issues certain types of people have, as part of a greater effort of helping those people.
